Who She Is

Two decades of turning pressure into purpose

Tress Larson began her career behind a salon chair at sixteen and spent over twenty years learning what truly drives success — not technique or hustle, but identity, presence, and the courage to lead from a place of deep self-worth.

Her path hasn't been without darkness. She's navigated abuse, struggled with body image, and fought her way back to clarity and faith. That journey is precisely what makes her work so transformative — she's not speaking from theory, but from a life rebuilt with intention.

Today, as a Global Education Director, Creative Director, and international keynote speaker, Tress helps ambitious women stop trading their peace for performance — and start building lives and businesses that feel as good as they look.
